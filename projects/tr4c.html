TR4-C

TR4-C was a group project completed for my Year 2 Mobile Game Development module. I led programming and assisted with the design of our game: a turn-based bot-battling RPG for Android, based on Medabots and partly inspired by Armored Core. I also managed source control for our team of five using Github.
